## General Preferences

### Event Timing
- **Prefer**: Evenings (after 6pm) or weekends
- **Okay with**: Afternoon events on weekends
- **Avoid**: Events after 11pm (too late)

### Price Range
- Willing to pay for quality events
- Sweet spot: $15-$50 per ticket
- Okay with higher prices for special artists ($50-$100)
- Also interested in free events

### Venue Types
- Intimate venues preferred over large stadiums
- Community centers and local spaces
- Galleries and cultural institutions
- **Avoid**: Sports arenas, nightclubs

### Other Considerations
- Prefer all-ages events, but 18+ is fine
- **Not interested in**:
  - Sports events (hockey, football, etc.)
  - Nightclub events
  - Festivals with mainly top 40 music
  - Corporate/networking events
  - Religious events

## Notes for EventFinder Agent

- When in doubt, **include it** - I'd rather see too many than miss something great
- Understand context: "The National" = indie rock band (not a holiday)
- "Blue Note" or "jazz club" = probably jazz music
- Workshop at craft store = hands-on class (probably interested)
- Be flexible with date parsing and time formats
- If event description is unclear, use venue type as a clue
