# Flyer Preferences

## Household
- 2 adults

## Dietary
- No restrictions

## Staples (always flag these when on sale)
- Classico tomato sauces
- Scotch bonnet peppers, peppers generally
- Milk, eggs, butter
- Siggi's plain yogurt
- Gorgonzola cheese
- Balderson's cheddar
- Swiss Delice dark chocolates
- Que Pasa corn chips
- No Name flour

## Also Interested In
- Meat & seafood deals (chicken, ground beef, salmon, pork)
- Produce (especially anything unusual or deeply discounted)
- Pantry staples on deep discount (rice, pasta, olive oil, canned tomatoes)

## Less Interested In
- Candy and Easter/seasonal chocolate
- Pharmacy / beauty / personal care
- Alcohol (skip liquor store flyers)
- Non-food items entirely
- Baby products
- Pet supplies

## What Makes a Good Deal
- Prefer items showing original price (real savings visible)
- Big discount % on staples > small discount on specialty items
- Bulk/family sizes are good
