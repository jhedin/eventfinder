# Domain Knowledge: Events

## Event Categories

### Music
- Concerts (pop, rock, classical, jazz, electronic)
- Festivals (multi-day, multi-artist)
- Open mic nights
- Album release parties

### Sports
- Professional games (NBA, NFL, MLB, NHL, MLS)
- College sports
- Amateur leagues
- Tournaments

### Arts & Culture
- Theater performances
- Ballet and dance
- Museum exhibitions
- Art gallery openings
- Film screenings

### Business & Professional
- Conferences
- Networking events
- Workshops and seminars
- Trade shows

### Social & Community
- Meetups
- Social clubs
- Community gatherings
- Fundraisers

## Event Attributes

### Required Information
- Event name
- Date and time
- Location (venue name and/or address)

### Desirable Information
- Category/type
- Description
- Price/ticket information
- Age restrictions
- Accessibility information
- Registration/RSVP details
- Organizer contact

## Common Search Patterns

Users typically search by:
1. **Type + Location** - "concerts in Austin"
2. **Type + Date** - "festivals this weekend"
3. **Specific performer/team** - "Taylor Swift tour dates"
4. **Location + Date** - "events in Chicago next month"
5. **Multiple criteria** - "free outdoor concerts in Portland this summer"

## Timezone Considerations

- Always clarify timezone when ambiguous
- Convert times to user's local timezone when known
- Display timezone abbreviations (PST, EST, etc.)
- Handle daylight saving time transitions
